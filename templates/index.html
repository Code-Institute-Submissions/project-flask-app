Getting started!

In FlexMongo you can create your own data collections. Do you want to keep track of your favorite movies? Planning to buy a new car and you want to store details on all your options? It is all possible in the FlexMongo app. You can even store photos and Youtube videos! Want to start your own project?

1 Create a new project

Create a new project and determine what information you want to store on your subject. You can add text fields when your input is going to be text, for example the model of a car. Add a number field when you want to store numbers, like car prices. Or a boolean field when you want to store a yes/no, for example if it is an electric car or not. You can store photos and videos later!

2 Add cases

Add cases to your project. Now it is time to fill your created collection with cases. In this section you can also add photos and videos. In order to store a photo you have to copy and paste the complete image URL. In order to store a Youtube vidoe you have to copy only the part of the URL after the 'v='. This will look somethin like this wv3oh5XV2yc.

3 View your project!

Once you have added some cases, you can view them by clicking on the project card. You can always add more cases later and you can even edit cases!
